Welcome to ThermoNED

an annual symposium on human thermoregulation and thermal environments

About

ThermoNED is an informal get-together for researchers in the Netherlands and bordering countries - we also have attendees from Germany, Belgium, France, UK, ... - and whoever is interested and/or involved in research on thermoregulation, environmental factors and health in humans and/or animals. It is a forum to exchange information on recent studies, results, and protocols: and to discuss and develop new research plans and establish collaborations.

When and where?

Each year, we change the location of the meeting to visit each other's labs, universities and cities. In 2025, we were hosted at the TU Delft. In 2026, we will celebrate the 20th edition of ThermoNED. The meeting will take place at Maastricht University, on the 17th of April 2026.

Who?

ThermoNED has some loyal ‘regulars’, but we are very much looking forward to meeting new people and to expand the group. Therefore, please feel free to suggest newcomers and to forward this information to others who might be interested. Please note that thermoregulation doesn’t always need to be the main focus – it is especially inspiring to discuss anything temperature-related from all different kinds of perspectives.
